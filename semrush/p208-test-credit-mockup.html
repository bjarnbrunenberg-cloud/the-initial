⚠ The First-Credit Problem

Users get 5 free searches. But the first one is inevitably a “test” — they type something random to see what results look like. That’s 20% of their free allowance gone on exploration, not real research. Users feel the loss immediately: “4/5 remaining” before they’ve even started. This creates regret and friction, especially for first-time visitors who don’t know what to expect.
